Place your PDF files here (minimum 10 PDFs, each ≥200 pages).

The ingestion pipeline will automatically:
- Extract native text using PyMuPDF
- Run OCR via Tesseract on scanned/image pages
- Chunk text into 800-token passages with 150-token overlap
- Embed using all-MiniLM-L6-v2
- Store in ChromaDB with HNSW index

After placing PDFs here, trigger ingestion via:
  - UI: Click "Ingest PDFs" button at http://localhost:8000
  - CLI: python scripts/ingest_and_query.py --pdf-dir ./data/pdfs
  - API: POST http://localhost:8000/ingest
